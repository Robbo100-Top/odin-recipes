Pancakes

Good Old Fashioned fluffy Pancakes!!

List of Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt (Just a pinch)
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Eggs

Cooking Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together. (Flour + Baking Powder + Sugar + Salt)
  2. Mix Well, then add the wet ingredients (Milk + Butter + Eggs)
  3. Stir these all together to combine
  4. Turn on Pan to heat up
  5. When at desired temperature, pour batter mix into hot pan
  6. Cook until bubbles begin to form on top and edges begin to set, then flip on to the other side
  7. Continue cooking until brown on both sides
  8. rinse and repeat until you have the desired amount of pancakes!!
  9. eat on their own or add a topping of your choosing

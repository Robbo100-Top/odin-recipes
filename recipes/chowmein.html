Chow Mein

Chow-Mein Noodles, containing Chicken and various vegetables

List of Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • ½ cup chopped mushrooms
  • 2 cups chopped celery
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • onions, chopped
  • 2 ½ cups chicken broth
  • 1 (15 ounce) can baby corn
  • ½ cup green beans
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ⅓ cup cold water
  • 3 cups cooked, cubed chicken meat

Cooking Steps

  1. In a Wok or skillet, melt butter over medium heat
  2. Add mushrooms, celery, onions, and garlic powder
  3. cook until the onions have wilted
  4. add chicken broth and baby corn
  5. continue to cook until celery is cooked yet still crisp
  6. stir in green beans/bean sprouts and soy sauce
  7. mix cornstarch and water in a small bowl
  8. slowly stir mixture into vegetables
  9. when sauce begins to thicken, mix in the chicken and heat through
  10. Plate and Enjoy
